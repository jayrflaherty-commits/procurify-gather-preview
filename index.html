🔥 "Pre-Purchase Budget Check"

Department leads need real-time budget visibility before initiating a purchase request — not after. 68% resort to Slack-asking their finance team. An embedded budget widget at the PO creation step could eliminate this entirely. Top-requested feature across all segments.

⚡ "Vendor Renewal Autopilot"

74% of mid-market teams miss SaaS renewal dates, auto-renewing at full price. Teams want automated alerts 90/60/30 days out with usage data attached. Currently a spreadsheet-and-calendar workflow. Massive retention differentiator.

📊 "Board-Ready Spend Reports"

CFOs spend 6+ hours/month manually building spend reports for board meetings. They want one-click export with variance commentary, category breakdowns, and trend lines. Directly impacts CFO champion advocacy.

Procurement Maturity Archetypes
📋 Spreadsheet Survivors (34%)

Managing all procurement in Excel/Google Sheets. Know they need a tool but overwhelmed by options. Highest pain, lowest sophistication. Procurify's easiest conversion target.

🔧 Franken-Stack Builders (27%)

Cobbled together QuickBooks + Expensify + shared folders. Functional but fragile. Breaking point is usually a failed audit or missed renewal. Ready for consolidation.

📈 Growing Pains (23%)

Recently scaled past 200 employees. Old processes crumbling. Often triggered by a new CFO hire who demands visibility. Highest urgency, fastest sales cycles.

🔄 Platform Switchers (16%)

Currently on Coupa, SAP Concur, or legacy P2P. Overpaying for features they don't use. Want to "right-size" their tool. Price-sensitive but high LTV once won.
